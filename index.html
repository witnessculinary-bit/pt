The Chef Behind the Table

A Story Told Through Food

Witness is a personal chef and caterer rooted in the belief that food is living cultural memory. Every dish carries the weight of tradition — the smoke of Southern kitchens, the spice of Lagos markets, the precision of Japanese technique, and the quiet power of plant-forward cooking.

Operating from Portugal, Witness creates bespoke culinary experiences for private dinners, weddings, corporate events, pop-up supper clubs, and cultural celebrations. Each menu is built from scratch for your occasion, your guests, your story.
